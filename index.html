Hello! I'm Nachiket.

I live in Atlanta & design web and mobile interfaces.

I am a designer by way of a scientist. I studied biology and worked in research for many years before deciding that my heart lay in design.

I currently lead design at Curri, a small education technology company. We are working on making the development bootcamp environment more productive and enjoyable for students and teachers.

I am interested in product design, and working on interesting ideas with interesting people. I believe that good typography makes a world of a difference, and that content comes first.

I am available for selected freelance work. You can take a look at some of my projects, follow me on twitter, or just shoot me an email to say hello.
